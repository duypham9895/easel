Menstrual — Days 1–5

Rest, release, and renewal. Energy is lower, introspection is high. She needs comfort, warmth, and gentle presence.

Inward turn, heightened sensitivity. She may need more reassurance and quieter evenings. Small gestures matter most.

The rhythm

Her four seasons, every month

Each phase has its own energy, needs, and gifts. Understanding all four is the key to understanding her — and making every month smoother than the last.

🌒

Menstrual

Days 1–5 · Rest phase
How she feels

Inward, tender, and tired. Energy is low, sensitivity is high. She craves warmth, softness, and permission to slow down without guilt.

How he can support

Take things off her plate. Bring the heating pad. Don't take her quietness personally — it's withdrawal to recharge, not withdrawal from you.

🌤️

Follicular

Days 6–13 · Rising phase
How she feels

Light, optimistic, and full of ideas. Energy climbs steadily. She's social, curious, and ready to say yes to things. This is her spark phase.

How he can support

Match her energy. Plan something she's been wanting to try. Be curious with her. Suggest the trip, the restaurant, the idea — she's ready for it.

🌕

Ovulation

Days 14–16 · Full bloom
How she feels

Confident, magnetic, and radiant. Peak communication and connection. She's at her most extroverted, most open, and most drawn to intimacy.

How he can support

Be present. Tell her she's beautiful (she'll believe it today). Plan date night here. Deep conversations happen naturally now — lean into them.

🌘

Luteal

Days 17–28 · Winding down
How she feels

Sensitive, introspective, and easily overwhelmed. The inner critic gets loud. Small things feel big. She needs reassurance more than solutions.

How he can support

Hold space without trying to fix. Less criticism, more validation. "I see you and you're doing great" goes further than any advice right now.
