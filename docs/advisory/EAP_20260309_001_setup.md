## Review Scope

This is a **feature-focused** expert review of the AI personalization approach in Easel. We are NOT reviewing the full app — only the specific feature that personalizes content based on Moon's daily check-in (mood + symptoms).

### What Changed

**Before:** Moon saw static i18n text for "About this Phase" and "Self-Care" sections (same text every day per phase). Sun got AI partner advice based on phase + day only — no awareness of Moon's actual mood/symptoms.

**After:** After Moon submits her daily check-in (mood 1-5 + up to 8 symptoms), AI generates:
1. **Personalized Phase Insight** (replaces static "About this Phase" card on Moon's dashboard)
2. **Personalized Self-Care** (replaces static "Self-Care" tip on Moon's dashboard)
3. **Mood-Aware Partner Advice** (Sun's "How to Show Up" card now includes Moon's actual mood/symptoms)

### Data Flow

```
Moon: DailyCheckIn → saves to daily_logs → triggers onCheckInSubmit callback
  ↓
Moon Dashboard: calls useAIPhaseInsight + useAISelfCare (parallel)
  ↓
Proxy: POST /api/phase-insight + POST /api/self-care
  ↓
MiniMax M2.5: generates personalized content
  ↓
Moon Dashboard: replaces static cards with AI content (FadeIn animation)

Sun Dashboard: fetches partner's daily_log → passes mood/symptoms to useAIPartnerAdvice
  ↓
Proxy: POST /api/partner-advice (now includes mood + symptoms)
  ↓
MiniMax M2.5: generates mood-aware advice
  ↓
Sun Dashboard: shows personalized advice in "How to Show Up" card
```

### AI Prompts (Key Design Decisions)

**Phase Insight prompt:**
- "Explain what is happening in her body during this phase in a warm, normalizing way"
- "Personalize based on mood and symptoms — connect what she's experiencing to what is typical"
- "Sound like a caring friend who knows biology, not a textbook"
- Max 50 words, temperature 0.8

**Self-Care prompt:**
- "Suggest ONE specific, actionable self-care activity"
- "Be concrete — not 'take care of yourself' but 'try a 10-minute warm bath with lavender tonight'"
- "Match the activity to her current state: gentle for low energy, active for high energy"
- Max 50 words, temperature 0.8

**Partner Advice prompt (updated):**
- When mood/symptoms available: "She checked in today: mood is '[label]', experiencing: [symptoms] — weave awareness of how she ACTUALLY feels into your advice"
- "Be concrete — not 'be supportive' but 'send her a voice note tonight instead of texting'"
- Max 45 words, temperature 0.8

### Fallback Pattern

All three features follow the established AI fallback pattern:
1. **Static content shown immediately** (i18n text from phases.ts)
2. **AI replaces on success** (with FadeIn animation for phase insight)
3. **Silent fallback on error** (static content stays, console.warn logged)

### Consent Model

- Moon's mood and symptoms are stored in `daily_logs` table
- Sun reads Moon's daily log via `fetchPartnerDailyLog()` using RLS (the `my_partner_id()` helper grants read access)
- Moon **has no explicit toggle** to control what Sun sees from her daily log
- The couple link itself implies consent to share cycle data

### Check-In UI

Moon's daily check-in collects:
- **Mood:** 5-point scale (1=Low, 2=Meh, 3=Okay, 4=Good, 5=Great)
- **Symptoms:** 8 options (Cramps, Bloating, Headache, Fatigue, Tender, Mood swings, Spotting, Cravings)
- Saved via upsert to `daily_logs` (idempotent — can update same day)
- On mount, checks if today's log exists and restores state + fetches AI
