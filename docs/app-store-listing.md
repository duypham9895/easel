## Description

Easel is a period tracking app designed for couples. She tracks her cycle — he stays informed and supportive. Together, you navigate every phase with empathy and understanding.

**For Her (Moon)**
- Track your menstrual cycle with smart predictions
- Log daily mood, symptoms, and energy levels
- Send Whisper signals to let your partner know how you feel
- Tap SOS when you need comfort — he'll know exactly what to do
- Sync with Apple Health for automatic cycle data
- Get personalized insights based on your current phase

**For Him (Sun)**
- Receive gentle, phase-aware guidance for every day of her cycle
- Get real-time Whisper and SOS alerts from your partner
- Understand what she needs — without her having to explain
- Learn about the four phases: menstrual, follicular, ovulatory, luteal
- Be the supportive partner you want to be

**Built for Two**
- Link with your partner using a simple couple code
- Real-time sync — changes appear instantly for both
- Dual themes: calming indigo for her, warm cream for him
- Available in English and Vietnamese

**Privacy First**
- Your health data stays yours — we never sell it
- End-to-end encrypted connections
- Row-level security on every database table
- HealthKit data processed locally on your device

Easel isn't just another period tracker. It's a bridge between partners — turning a private experience into shared understanding.

## Review Notes
Easel is a couples period tracking app with two roles:
- Moon (girlfriend): Tracks her menstrual cycle, mood, and symptoms. Can send Whisper signals and SOS alerts.
- Sun (boyfriend): Receives phase-aware guidance and real-time alerts from his partner.

To test the app:
1. Create two accounts (one for each role)
2. Moon logs her cycle start date
3. Sun enters Moon's couple code to link
4. Both users can now see shared cycle data

The app requires two users to experience the full couple features. Single-user cycle tracking works for the Moon role without linking.

AI features require network connectivity and may take a few seconds to load — static fallback content is shown immediately.
